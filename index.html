Our Menu

Chicken

Embark on a flavor adventure with our Sizzling Fajita Fiesta. Tender strips of chicken breast, marinated in a zesty blend of lime, chili, and cumin, are grilled to perfection alongside vibrant bell peppers and onions. Served on a cast-iron skillet with warm flour tortillas, fluffy rice, and a trio of house-made salsas, this dish invites you to create your own personalized fajita masterpiece.

Beef

Embark on a culinary journey to the heart of France with our Bourguignon Rhapsody. Tender chunks of beef, slow-braised in red wine with aromatic herbs, bacon lardons, and earthy mushrooms, create a symphony of flavors that is both comforting and sophisticated. Served with creamy mashed potatoes, this dish is a timeless classic that captures the essence of French country cooking.

Sushi

Unleash your adventurous side with our Dragon Roll. This visually stunning creation features succulent shrimp tempura, creamy avocado, and crisp cucumber, all wrapped in nori and topped with a vibrant mosaic of thinly sliced avocado and a drizzle of sweet eel sauce. With its harmonious blend of textures and flavors, this roll is a feast for both the eyes and the palate.
