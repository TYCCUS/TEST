Our Menu

Chicken

Sizzling Fajita Fiesta Embark on a flavor adventure with our Sizzling Fajita Fiesta. Tender strips of chicken breast, marinated in a zesty blend of lime, chili, and cumin, are grilled to perfection alongside vibrant bell peppers and onions. Served on a cast-iron skillet with warm flour tortillas, fluffy rice, and a trio of house-made salsas, this dish invites you to create your own personalized fajita masterpiece. Lemon Herb Roasted Chicken Indulge in the comforting simplicity of our Lemon Herb Roasted Chicken. A whole chicken, infused with aromatic herbs and bright lemon zest, is slow-roasted until its skin turns golden brown and its meat becomes incredibly tender. Served with a medley of roasted root vegetables and a drizzle of pan jus, this dish is a celebration of classic flavors and wholesome ingredients.

Beef

Cowboy Ribeye Unleash your inner carnivore with our Cowboy Ribeye. This massive, bone-in ribeye steak, expertly aged and seasoned, is grilled to your desired temperature, showcasing its rich marbling and robust flavor. Served with a side of crispy onion rings and a dollop of creamy horseradish sauce, this dish is a true testament to the primal pleasure of a perfectly cooked steak. Bourguignon Rhapsody Embark on a culinary journey to the heart of France with our Bourguignon Rhapsody. Tender chunks of beef, slow-braised in red wine with aromatic herbs, bacon lardons, and earthy mushrooms, create a symphony of flavors that is both comforting and sophisticated. Served with creamy mashed potatoes, this dish is a timeless classic that captures the essence of French country cooking.

Sushi

Dragon Roll Unleash your adventurous side with our Dragon Roll. This visually stunning creation features succulent shrimp tempura, creamy avocado, and crisp cucumber, all wrapped in nori and topped with a vibrant mosaic of thinly sliced avocado and a drizzle of sweet eel sauce. With its harmonious blend of textures and flavors, this roll is a feast for both the eyes and the palate. Omakase Extravaganza Surrender to the artistry of our sushi chefs with the Omakase Extravaganza. This multi-course tasting menu showcases the freshest seasonal ingredients and the most innovative sushi techniques. From delicate nigiri to intricate maki rolls, each dish is a testament to the chef's passion and skill. Embark on a culinary adventure that will tantalize your taste buds and leave you craving more.
